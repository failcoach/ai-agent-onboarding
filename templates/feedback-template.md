# Feedback Template

Use this when you want to give your agent meaningful feedback that changes future behavior. You don't need this for quick corrections ("make this shorter," "wrong tone"). Use it when you notice a pattern or want to make a lasting adjustment.

For the reasoning behind good feedback, the 24-hour rule, and the weekly check-in, see [guides/04-feedback.md](../guides/04-feedback.md). If your agent keeps missing the same thing despite feedback, check the "When Feedback Isn't Working" section in that guide.

### What I Asked For

[The task, output, or behavior you're reviewing.]

### What Worked

[Be specific. What was good about the output? This matters as much as the corrections. The agent needs to know what to keep doing.]

### What Didn't Work

[Be specific. What was wrong, missing, or off? "It wasn't great" doesn't help. "The recommendations were too generic and didn't account for our budget constraint of $5K/month" does.]

### Why This Matters

[Help the agent understand the reasoning behind the correction. "Our customers are price-sensitive, so any recommendation that assumes unlimited budget will always miss the mark."]

### What to Do Differently

[Clear instruction for next time. Specific enough that the agent could follow it without guessing.]

### Action Needed

- [ ] No permanent change needed (just session feedback)
- [ ] Update the agent's instructions (add this as a rule or guideline)
- [ ] Update the agent's memory (new context or changed facts)

### Notes
